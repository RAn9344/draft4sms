WhatsApp API Onboarding & KYC Requirements

To begin using the Official WhatsApp Business API via Draft4SMS, businesses are required to complete a short but mandatory verification and onboarding process. This ensures compliance with Meta (Facebook) policies and enables access to all WhatsApp Business API features, including green tick verification, broadcast messaging, and automation

Step-by-Step: How to Get Started

Create a Facebook Business Manager Account

You must have a verified Facebook Business Manager account to link your WhatsApp number and manage messaging limits.

Submit KYC Documents

Provide business verification documents to activate your WhatsApp Business profile:

  • GST or MSME Certificate (Complete and verified)
  • Company PAN Card (or Owner PAN for proprietorships)
  • Domain Name (Used for branded sender name)
  • Active Business Facebook Page

Verify Domain Name

Domain verification is essential for displaying your brand name and increasing credibility.

Letter of Authorization (LOA)

Required only if the WhatsApp number is different from the registered domain entity. Must be printed on letterhead, signed, and stamped.
